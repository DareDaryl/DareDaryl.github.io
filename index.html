Daryl Shifflett


I’ve always loved coding. Over time, I’ve come to understand that with enough curiosity, focus, and guidance, almost anything can be learned. At one point in my career as an Operations Manager, I hit a plateau. I found myself craving a new challenge. Something that would engage the creative and problem-solving sides of my brain. During this period of reflection, I started researching engineering programs and stumbled upon software development bootcamps that seemed both rigorous and exciting. After a month of exploring my options, I chose a full-time, six-month full-stack coding bootcamp—40 hours a week of immersive, hands-on learning. That decision changed everything. I haven’t looked back since.
